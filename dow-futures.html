How to trade Dow futures in India

Accessing US futures from India — brokers, contract types, trading hours and practical checklists.

Overview

Dow futures (E-mini or full contracts) allow exposure to the Dow Jones Industrial Average. Traders in India can access them via international brokers or via local brokers that offer international derivatives/CFDs. Pay attention to margin, currency conversion and settlement.

Key considerations

  • Broker selection: international access, regulatory standing, fees.
  • Margin and leverage: understand maintenance margins and potential for big moves overnight.
  • Trading hours and overlaps with Indian market hours.
