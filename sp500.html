How to trade S&P 500 in India

Exposure methods, margin & currency points to consider when trading the S&P 500 from India.

Overview

The S&P 500 is a widely followed US equity index. Indian traders can access it using E-mini futures, ETFs (like SPY), or CFDs depending on broker access and local rules.

Key considerations

  • Choose instrument based on cost, liquidity and taxes (futures vs ETFs vs CFDs).
  • Beware of currency risk — INR/USD moves affect returns.
  • Understand US session volatility and major economic event impacts.

Strategy tips

  • Use macro economic calendar to avoid trading into major US data releases without preparedness.
  • Consider using options on ETFs or futures for defined-risk strategies.
